# shopping-behavior

## Analytical Questions

What are 6 analytical questions you can ask about this dataset?

1. Which age groups generate the highest total purchase revenue, and how does their average purchase amount compare?

2. How does purchase behavior vary by season across different U.S. locations (states)?

3. Do customers who use promo codes spend more or less per transaction compared to those who don’t?

4. Which combinations of item type, size, and color are most frequently purchased together?

5. How does the number of previous purchases relate to customer spending and purchase frequency?

6. Which payment methods are most associated with higher purchase amounts?
